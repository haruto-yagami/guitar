Integrated Acoustic Guitar Learning Path

Level 1: Foundations (Beginner Focus)
  • Guitar Basics
    • Guitar anatomy review
    • Holding pick, posture, tuning basics
  • Open Chords
    • C, G, D, Em, Am, A, E
    • Basic chord changes practice
  • Strumming & Rhythm
    • Downstrokes and simple down-up strumming
    • Basic rhythm and counting (4/4 time)
  • First Songs
    • Simple 2-3 chord songs to build confidence
  • Practice Techniques
    • Metronome use, slow practice, clean transitions
Level 2: Technique & Theory Essentials
  • Finger Dexterity
    • Chromatic exercises, finger independence drills
  • Fingerpicking Introduction
    • Basic Travis picking, alternating bass patterns
  • Music Theory Basics
    • Notes on fretboard (low E and A strings)
    • Intervals and chord construction
    • Major scale, pentatonic scale introduction
  • Ear Training & Improvisation
    • Identify major/minor chords
    • Simple melodic improvisation
Level 3: Chord Expansion & Stylization
  • Barre Chords Fundamentals
    • F major, B minor shapes and movable shapes
  • Advanced Chords
    • 7th chords (maj7, m7, dom7), sus chords
  • Rhythmic Complexity
    • Syncopation, muted strums, accents
  • Combined Techniques
    • Integrating fingerpicking with strumming
  • Playing Riffs & Fills
    • Common acoustic riffs, intros, and melodic phrases
Level 4: Song Mastery & Jamming Skills
  • Full Song Learning
    • Play songs with open and barre chords across genres
    • Learn acoustic favorites and popular hits
  • Jamming Tools
    • Call-and-response exercises
    • Using backing tracks and play-alongs
    • Improvisation over chord progressions
  • Advanced Ear Training
    • Transcribing melodies and chord progressions
  • Musician Mindset
    • Introduction to alternate tunings
    • Songwriting ideas and arrangement basics
    • Recording and self-evaluation
Level 5: Advanced Styles & Mastery
  • Fingerstyle Variations and Techniques
  • Complex Rhythms and Time Signatures
  • Advanced Soloing and Lead Guitar Concepts
  • Genre-specific courses: Blues, Folk, Pop, etc.
  • Creative Expression & Performance Skills
